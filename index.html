About Inturo

After five years of teaching with varying levels of professional development the value of teacher support was imparted to me. As a designer with a focus on ed tech I want to create an app that can help new teachers succeed. Through interviews, research I have come to this first iteration of the “Intruo”.

“Inturo” is a teacher reflection/coaching web-based application. With a focus on helping new teachers stay in the teaching field for more than three years, and making new teachers’ better teachers through, shared resources, and reflection journaling, and lesson plan creation..
